Meet Jeery

Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated mammals known for their loyalty and intelligence, making them one of humanity's most beloved companions. With a life span ranging from 10 to 15 years, their size, coat, and temperament vary widely depending on the breed. As social animals, dogs thrive on companionship and are highly trainable, capable of understanding commands and even human emotions.
